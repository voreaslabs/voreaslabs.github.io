About VLI

VLI is a DARPA-funded technology spinoff from the Georgia Institute of Technology's Center for Cyber Operations Enquiry and Unconventional Sensing (COEUS). VLI helps government agencies and systemically important organizations identify and track major nation state and criminal cyber actors across the Internet. VLI provides continuous, scalable, and oblivious-to-adversaries tracking of attack infrastructure affecting networks of interest.
